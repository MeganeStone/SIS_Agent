Constitutional AI

A Novel Approach to AI Safety

Constitutional AI is Anthropic's innovative method for training AI systems to be safe and helpful without extensive human feedback.

  • Self-Critique: AI models learn to evaluate and improve their own responses
  • Constitutional Principles: A set of guiding rules inspired by human values
  • Scalable Oversight: Reduces reliance on human labeling for safety training
  • Transparent Reasoning: Models can explain why they made certain decisions
